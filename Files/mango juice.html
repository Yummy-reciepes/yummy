Mango Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 medium mangoes or 1 large or large 1⁄2 large cups frozen or canned pulp.
  • 1⁄2 cups to 2cups chilled water or coconut water from young coconuts or orange juice.
  • 3 tablespoons sugar or manuka honey(adjust to taste)
  • 1 1⁄2 inch ginger piece.

    • Optional

  • 5 to 6 mint leaves for flavour
  • 1 inch ginger(fresh root)
  • 1 lemon or lime
  • 2 tbsps chia seeds or sabja seeds.

Tools Required

  • 1 cup 250ml
  • Blender
  • 1 Table Spoon

Time preparation

10 Mins

Servings

3 Times

Method

  1. Wash mango thoroughly and chill them in the fridge .peel the skin
  2. Cut the mango to cubes and add them to a blender.
  3. Pour the coconut water or orange juice or water.Add sugar and ice cubes.If using Ice cubes.reduce the liquid.
  4. Optional-Put in the other ingredients if using like ginger and mint.
  5. Blend until smooth.Pour more liquid if needed to your taste.Serve immmediately.
  6. If using chia seeds or sabja seeds,then rinse them thoroughly in a strainer.Soak in 1cup water and set aside for atleast 20 mins to 2 hours.
  7. Stir them in the juice just before serving.
  8. Serve mango juice immediately.
