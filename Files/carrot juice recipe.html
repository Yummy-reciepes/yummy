Carrot Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 grams carrots fresh & tender.
  • 1⁄2 lemon small,(skip if using sour apples)
  • 1 cup water(if making in a blender).
  • 1 1⁄2 inch ginger piece.
  • 3 oranges or 2 to 3 pears or 5 to 6 tangerians or 2 lemons.

Tools Required

  • 1 cup 250ml
  • Blender

Time preparation

10 Mins

Servings

2 Times

Method

    Preparation

  1. Wash carrots under running water.Use a vegetable scrub or brush to clean them,Rinse Well.
  2. Soak them in a large pot fiiled with clean water for 30 mins.
  3. If desired peel off the skin.
  4. Wash and peel oranges.Desired and halve them.
  5. If making in mixer or blender,cube them to 1 inch pieces. Add them to blender jar along with other ingredients.
  6. If making in juicer ,then cut to desired sizes to suit your juicer chute.

    7. How to make Carrot juice

  7. Run the machine.
  8. If making in mixer or blender,the pass through a strainer/filter and collect the juice at the bottom.
  9. Serve carrot juice fresh and immediately.
