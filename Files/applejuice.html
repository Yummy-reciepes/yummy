Apple Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 Apples
  • 1/2 lemon small
  • 1/2 ginger piece
  • 1 cup water or orange juice as needed(if making in a blender)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 handful holy basil(tulsi)or mint leaves(pudina)

Instructions

  1. Wash Apples thoroughly under running water
  2. if you prefer to use the skin,then make sure they are at room tempeature and spray vinegar.vinegar.set these aside for 5mins.rub them gently with salt and rinse off under running water
  3. cut the chunks of apples and dicard the seeds
  4. add them a chunks of apples to blender
  5. blend the apples and add water until the consistency
  6. serve appple juice fresh immediately
